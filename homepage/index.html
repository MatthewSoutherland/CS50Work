About myself

I graduated from the University of Northwester Ohio in 2006, with a double diploma in automotive technology and heavy truck repair. I worked for Firestone as a machanic then I got a better job working for Waste Management. After a few years of my body taking a beating, I realized that I needed to learn a new skill. The older guys were wearing back braces on vicodin and could hardly move. I then got a job with Merrill Technologies, the company im currently at after 15 years. Merrill is a diversified manufactering company that produces world class, state of the art technolgy. With a lot of hard work I have become a senior machinist/programmer. No one would believe my responsabilities if I shared them, so I wont. I will say that Merrill is an amazing company with some of the best talent on the planet.

Its because of these values instilled in me that I have been able to be successful in this world. At 38 years old I am married with two boys. My oldest son Garrett is 13 years old and in NJHS. Im looking forward to becoming a full time software engineer and having more time with family.
